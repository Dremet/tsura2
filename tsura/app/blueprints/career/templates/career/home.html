Welcome to TSU Career — a seasonal championship on the TSURA Career server. Everyone starts a season in the same base car and earns credits by racing (the slower you finish, the more you get, to keep the field close). Spend credits on the website garage to upgrade your car's Top Speed, Acceleration, Braking and Downforce. Your tuned car is generated and forced onto you automatically at race night. Championship points are separate from credits.

Races run weekly on the Career server — two races per evening, each with a short qualifying. Career results do not affect ELO.
