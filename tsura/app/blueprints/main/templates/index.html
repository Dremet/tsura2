Turbo Sliders Unlimited Racing Association (TSURA) is a community-driven initiative focused on organized competitive racing in Turbo Sliders Unlimited. It goes beyond just this website and the data processing behind it — TSURA also involves event planning, community-building, ELO-based rankings, custom servers and more.
This site showcases live statistics gathered from the Event, Hotlapping and TripleHeat server. For questions or bugs, please contact Dremet on Discord.
